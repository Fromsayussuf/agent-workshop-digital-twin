Can Özfuttu is a software engineer and entrepreneur working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, blockchain, and distributed systems.

He is the co-founder of Fluton, where he focuses on building next-generation cross-chain infrastructure and confidential execution systems. His work combines system design, smart contracts, and AI-driven architectures.

He is also working as a software engineer at Karakod and is actively developing Legalifi, an AI-powered platform designed to analyze legal and corporate documents. Legalifi allows organizations to automatically extract key clauses, identify potential risks, and gain structured insights from complex documentation.

Can has a strong technical background in full-stack development, blockchain protocols, and AI systems. Over the years, he has worked on complex projects involving cross-chain communication, intent-based architectures, and AI-powered applications.

He is particularly interested in building products that solve real-world problems and push the boundaries of privacy-preserving technologies. His work often explores how AI and cryptography can be combined to create more secure and intelligent systems.

In addition to his engineering work, Can is actively involved in designing new product ideas, leading technical strategy, and building scalable software platforms.
